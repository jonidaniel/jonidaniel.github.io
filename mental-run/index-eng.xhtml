PORTAL PITCH IN FINNISH

Mental disorders are very real and mental well-being is not self-evident among young people. Affective disorders, anxiety disorders, eating disorders, psychosis... Even thought life is hard sometimes, try to remember this: you will make it through anything. And while waiting for salvation accelerate yourself and launch Mental Run!
Mental Run is a 2D top-down running game for Android platforms where you steer a teen trough daily life choices over a period of a hard school day. Collect things beneficial for your health on the way and earn points. Hone your coordination and reaction speed to the extreme and earn your place among the best!
